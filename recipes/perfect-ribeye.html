The Perfect Ribeye

Ribeye is what we're tackling today. So stay tuned in order to learn how to cook the perfect ribeye. YUM!

First off, let's go over what you'll need:

  • Cut of ribeye meat (preferably something more fatty or with more marbling)
  • Salt
  • Garlic powder
  • Butter
  • *Optional* Chopped up garlic

And that's pretty much all we need to make the most basic ribeye that's still pretty bomb.

Now, let's move into how to actually fry this joint up!

  1. Wash your ribeye. (I don't care if you're about to fry that shit up, just make sure to wash it. Be clean people.)
  2. Pat dry and then start sprinkling a generous amount of salt and garlic powder on it. Don't forget to get the sides of the steak!
  3. Cut up the garlic. Throw some oil in the pan.
  4. When it starts to get hot, throw the minced garlic in there. When it has a golden brown tint, throw the steak in the pan.
  5. Sear each side AND the sides until you have a nice golden brown crust.
  6. At this point, throw in the butter. Let it melt and start basting the steak.
  7. When the steak's internal temperature reachs 135 degrees, take it out and rest it on a plate for about 2-5 minutes.
  8. Enjoy that shit.
